Quick Start

Up and running in three steps.

1

Download & Extract

Click Download Template above. Extract the ZIP to your project folder.

2

Configure main.tex

Edit main.tex — set your profile, language, and institution in the \documentclass line.

3

Compile

Run latexmk -xelatex main.tex or use your preferred editor (Overleaf, VS Code, TeXstudio).
