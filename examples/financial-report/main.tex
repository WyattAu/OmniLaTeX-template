\section*{Letter to Shareholders}

Dear Shareholders,

The first half of fiscal year 2026 delivered strong operational and financial
performance across all business segments. Consolidated revenue reached
\$247.8M, representing 16.3\% year-over-year growth driven by robust demand
in our Enterprise Solutions division and continued expansion in international
markets.

Operating income grew 22.1\% to \$48.6M, reflecting disciplined cost
management and favorable product mix. Net income per diluted share was \$1.87,
up from \$1.52 in the prior-year period. Free cash flow generation of
\$52.3M provided ample resources for strategic investments while maintaining
a conservative balance sheet with \$184.2M in cash and equivalents.

During the quarter, we completed the acquisition of DataStream Analytics for
\$62M, strengthening our capabilities in real-time data processing. We also
launched our next-generation platform, NovaCore 4.0, which received strong
early adoption with 34 enterprise customers in the first six weeks.

\vspace{1em}
\noindent\textbf{Robert Harrington}\\
Chief Executive Officer\\
June 2026

\subsection{Enterprise Solutions}

Enterprise Solutions delivered record revenue of \$142.5M, up 22.4\%
year-over-year. Key drivers included:

\begin{itemize}
    \item NovaCore 4.0 launch contributing \$8.2M in incremental revenue
          with 34 new enterprise customers.
    \item Renewal rate of 94\% with net revenue retention of 118\%,
          reflecting strong cross-sell into existing accounts.
    \item Three seven-figure deals closed with Fortune 500 companies in
          healthcare and financial services verticals.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Cloud \& Platform}

Cloud \& Platform revenue grew 28.1\% to \$58.4M, the fastest-growing
segment. Highlights:

\begin{itemize}
    \item Annual recurring revenue (ARR) reached \$112M, up 31\% from
          \$85.6M at the same point last year.
    \item Platform usage (compute hours) increased 42\% as customers
          expanded AI/ML workloads.
    \item Gross margin improved to 72.3\% from 68.1\% due to infrastructure
          optimization and reserved capacity planning.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Professional Services}

Professional Services revenue grew 12.3\% to \$32.9M, supported by
implementation demand for NovaCore 4.0 and DataStream integration projects.
The services backlog increased to \$48.2M, providing strong forward
visibility.

\section{Risk Factors}

\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Macroeconomic uncertainty.} Persistent inflation and
        elevated interest rates may reduce enterprise technology spending.
        Current exposure is mitigated by multi-year contracts representing
        78\% of ARR.
    \item \textbf{Competitive pressure.} Cloud hyperscalers continue to
        expand bundled AI capabilities. Our differentiation through
        vertical solutions and integration depth remains strong, but
        requires sustained R\&D investment.
    \item \textbf{Integration risk.} The DataStream acquisition, closed in
        Q1 2026, adds 120 employees and 45 enterprise customers.
        Integration is on track but carries execution risk through the
        next two quarters.
    \item \textbf{Regulatory environment.} Evolving AI regulations in the
        EU and US may require additional compliance investments. We have
        allocated \$3.2M in FY2026 for governance tooling and legal review.
\end{enumerate}
