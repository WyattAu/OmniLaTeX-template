\section*{Welcome}

Welcome to DevConnect Summit 2026! This year's theme, \emph{Building the
Future}, brings together over 2,000 developers, architects, and technology
leaders for two days of talks, workshops, and networking. We are excited to
host you in San Francisco and hope you leave inspired and connected.
