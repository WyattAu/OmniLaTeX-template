\section*{Welcome to NovaPulse Technologies}

Welcome to NovaPulse. This handbook is your guide to our culture, policies,
benefits, and the resources available to you as a member of our team. We
designed this document to answer the questions you are most likely to have as
you join or grow with us.

NovaPulse is building the future of industrial predictive maintenance. Our
mission is to eliminate preventable downtime through intelligent,
data-driven systems. We believe that the best products are built by teams
that are supported, trusted, and empowered to do their best work.

Please read this handbook carefully and keep it accessible for future reference.
If anything is unclear, your manager or the People Operations team
(\href{mailto:people@novapulse.ai}{people@novapulse.ai}) can help.

\section{Our Values}

\begin{description}
    \item[Safety First.] We protect people, assets, and data. Every decision
        begins with the question: ``Is this safe?''
    \item[Ownership.] We act like founders. If you see a problem, it is your
        problem to solve.
    \item[Curiosity.] We ask questions, challenge assumptions, and pursue
        understanding. Intellectual honesty is expected at every level.
    \item[Impact Over Activity.] We measure outcomes, not hours logged.
        Busywork has no place here.
    \item[Transparency.] We share information openly, default to
        ``everyone-in'' on context, and communicate honestly---even when the
        news is difficult.
\end{description}

\section{Employment Policies}

\subsection{At-Will Employment}

NovaPulse employs at-will in all jurisdictions where we operate. Either you
or the company may end the employment relationship at any time, with or
without cause or notice, subject to applicable law.

\subsection{Equal Opportunity}

We are committed to providing equal employment opportunities to all qualified
individuals regardless of race, color, religion, sex, sexual orientation,
gender identity, national origin, age, disability, genetic information,
veteran status, or any other legally protected characteristic.

\subsection{Anti-Harassment \& Anti-Discrimination}

NovaPulse maintains a zero-tolerance policy for harassment and discrimination
of any kind. This includes, but is not limited to:

\begin{itemize}
    \item Verbal, written, or physical harassment based on protected
          characteristics.
    \item Unwelcome sexual advances, requests for sexual favors, or other
          conduct of a sexual nature.
    \item Retaliation against any employee who reports harassment or
          participates in an investigation.
\end{itemize}

Report concerns immediately to your manager, People Operations, or through the
anonymous ethics hotline at \href{mailto:ethics@novapulse.ai}{ethics@novapulse.ai}.

\subsection{Working Hours}

\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Core hours:} 10:00 AM -- 3:00 PM in your local time zone.
          All team meetings are scheduled within this window.
    \item \textbf{Flexible hours:} Beyond core hours, you are free to structure
          your workday as you see fit, subject to manager agreement.
    \item \textbf{Full-time status:} 40 hours per week. Overtime is not
          expected and not compensated unless required by applicable law.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Remote Work}

\begin{itemize}
    \item NovaPulse is a remote-first company. Most employees work from home.
    \item You may work from any location within your country of employment.
    \item International remote work (up to 30 days per year) requires
          advance approval from People Operations and Legal.
    \item You are responsible for maintaining a safe, ergonomic workspace.
          The company provides a \$1,500 home office stipend upon hire and
          a \$500 annual refresh allowance.
\end{itemize}

\section{Code of Conduct}

\subsection{Professional Standards}

All employees are expected to:

\begin{enumerate}
    \item Act with integrity in all business dealings.
    \item Comply with all applicable laws, regulations, and company policies.
    \item Protect confidential information---both company and customer data.
    \item Avoid conflicts of interest, or disclose them promptly when they
          arise.
    \item Treat colleagues, customers, partners, and community members with
          respect and professionalism.
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{Confidentiality \& IP}

\begin{itemize}
    \item All work product created during your employment belongs to NovaPulse.
    \item You may not share proprietary information with external parties
          without written authorization from Legal.
    \item Upon departure, you must return all company property and delete
          company data from personal devices within 48 hours.
    \item Non-disclosure obligations survive termination of employment
          indefinitely.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Acceptable Use of Technology}

\begin{itemize}
    \item Company devices and accounts are for business purposes. Limited
          personal use is permitted provided it does not interfere with
          work, violate policies, or create security risks.
    \item Do not install unauthorized software on company devices. Submit
          requests through IT.
    \item Use strong, unique passwords and enable multi-factor authentication
          on all accounts.
    \item Report suspected security incidents immediately to
          \href{mailto:security@novapulse.ai}{security@novapulse.ai}.
\end{itemize}

\section{Performance \& Growth}

\subsection{Performance Reviews}

We conduct performance reviews on a semi-annual basis (June and December).
Reviews include:

\begin{itemize}
    \item Self-assessment completed by you.
    \item Manager assessment with written feedback.
    \item Peer feedback from 3--5 colleagues you select.
    \item Calibration session with leadership to ensure consistency.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Promotion Process}

\begin{enumerate}
    \item Nominations accepted from managers or self-nominations during
          open nomination periods (March and September).
    \item Promotion committee reviews nominations against published level
          frameworks.
    \item Decisions communicated within 30 days of committee review.
    \item Effective date of promotion: the first pay period following
          approval.
\end{enumerate}

\section{Health \& Safety}

\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Ergonomic assessment.} Complete the self-assessment within
          your first week. Request a professional virtual assessment if
          needed through People Operations.
    \item \textbf{Emergency procedures.} For in-office or co-working space
          employees, review the posted evacuation plans. For remote employees,
          ensure you have a safe working environment and know local emergency
          numbers.
    \item \textbf{Incident reporting.} Report any work-related injury or
          safety concern to People Operations within 24 hours.
    \item \textbf{Mental health resources.} Lyra Health therapy sessions,
          EAP counseling, and manager training on mental health awareness
          are all available at no cost to you.
\end{itemize}

\section{Additional Policies}

\begin{description}
    \item[Expense Policy.] Submit expenses within 30 days via Ramp. Items
        under \$100 do not require receipts. Items over \$100 require
        itemized receipts. Manager approval required for expenses over
        \$500.
    \item[Travel Policy.] Economy class for flights under 4 hours; premium
        economy permitted for 4+ hours. Hotel booking through Concur.
        Per diem: \$75/day domestic, \$125/day international.
    \item[Relocation.] Relocation assistance available on a case-by-case
        basis. Typical package includes moving expenses up to \$10,000,
        a temporary housing stipend of \$2,500, and travel for house-hunting
        trips.
    \item[Referral Bonus.] \$5,000 for engineering and product referrals;
        \$3,000 for all other roles. Paid 50\% at hire and 50\% at
        6-month retention.
\end{description}
