\section*{NovaPulse Technologies Surpasses \$50M ARR, Launches Multi-Region Platform to Serve European Enterprise Customers}

\noindent\textbf{SAN FRANCISCO --- June 16, 2026 ---} NovaPulse Technologies,
Inc., the leading AI-powered predictive maintenance platform for industrial
manufacturing, today announced it has surpassed \$50 million in annual
recurring revenue (ARR), added 42 enterprise customers in Q2 2026, and will
launch full multi-region platform capabilities in Europe by November 2026.

The milestones underscore NovaPulse's rapid growth trajectory and expanding
role as the platform of record for predictive maintenance across
manufacturing, healthcare, and energy verticals.

\section*{Surpassing \$50M ARR}

NovaPulse reached the \$50M ARR milestone in June 2026, growing 42\%
year-over-year. The company added 42 new enterprise customers during Q2,
including Meridian Healthcare, Nordic Energy Corp, and Apex Pharmaceuticals.
Average contract value increased to \$122,000, reflecting deeper platform
adoption within existing accounts.

\begin{quote}
``\textit{Surpassing \$50M ARR in just over two years of commercial
launch is a testament to the urgency of the problem we solve and the
depth of our platform. Manufacturers are tired of reactive maintenance
and alert fatigue---they want intelligent, prescriptive systems that
predict failures before they happen. That is exactly what NovaPulse
delivers.''}
\end{quote}

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent--- \textbf{Dr.\ Elena Vasquez}, CEO and Co-Founder, NovaPulse Technologies

\section*{Multi-Region Platform Launch}

Project Atlas, NovaPulse's initiative to deploy a fully operational European
region, is on track for general availability in November 2026. The EU region,
hosted in AWS Frankfurt (eu-central-1), will provide:

\begin{itemize}
    \item Sub-100ms API response times for customers in Western Europe.
    \item Full data residency controls enabling customers to restrict data
          processing and storage to the EU region.
    \item Active-active multi-region failover with 99.99\% uptime SLA.
    \item Edge inference nodes deployed in European data centers for
          low-latency ML inference on industrial sensor data.
\end{itemize}

Six enterprise customers, including Nordic Energy Corp and two unnamed Fortune
500 manufacturers, have committed to beta testing the EU region ahead of
general availability.

\begin{quote}
``\textit{Data residency and low-latency performance are non-negotiable for
our operations. NovaPulse's multi-region platform gives us the compliance
confidence and performance we need to scale predictive maintenance across
our European facilities.''}
\end{quote}

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent--- \textbf{Thomas Lindqvist}, VP of Operations, Nordic Energy Corp

\section*{Strategic Partnership with Siemens Digital Industries}

NovaPulse also announced an expanded partnership with Siemens Digital
Industries to co-develop next-generation predictive maintenance solutions
for the Siemens MindSphere IoT platform. The collaboration will integrate
NovaPulse's AI inference engine with Siemens' industrial automation
ecosystem, enabling joint customers to deploy predictive maintenance
capabilities directly within their existing Siemens infrastructure.

\begin{quote}
``\textit{Siemens and NovaPulse share a vision of making predictive
maintenance accessible and actionable for every manufacturer. This
partnership combines Siemens' deep industrial automation expertise with
NovaPulse's cutting-edge AI platform. Together, we can help customers
reduce unplanned downtime by 40--50\%.''}
\end{quote}

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent--- \textbf{Dr.\ Klaus Weber}, Executive VP, Siemens Digital Industries

\section*{Product Innovation}

During Q2 2026, NovaPulse shipped several significant product updates:

\begin{description}
    \item[NovaCore 4.1.] Advanced workflow automation enabling customers to
        build custom maintenance orchestration pipelines without code. Early
        adopters report 35\% faster time-to-value compared to manual
        configuration.
    \item[Edge SDK v2.2.] Enhanced support for NVIDIA Jetson Orin and
        ARM-based industrial gateways, expanding the range of edge hardware
        compatible with NovaPulse inference models.
    \item[AI Explainability Dashboard.] Real-time feature attribution for
        all predictive models, providing the transparency required by
        regulated industries (healthcare, financial services, government).
    \item[Snowflake Integration.] Native data export to Snowflake data
        warehouses, enabling customers to combine NovaPulse analytics with
        their existing business intelligence stack. 28 customers activated
        the integration within the first six weeks.
\end{description}

\section*{About NovaPulse Technologies}

NovaPulse Technologies is the leading AI-powered predictive maintenance
platform for industrial manufacturing. Founded in 2024 by Dr.\ Elena Vasquez
and Marcus Chen, NovaPulse combines edge computing, machine learning, and
digital twin technology to predict equipment failures before they happen,
reducing unplanned downtime by up to 45\% and extending asset lifespan by
20--30\%.

NovaPulse serves over 280 enterprise customers across manufacturing,
healthcare, and energy verticals, monitoring more than 180,000 industrial
assets globally. The company is headquartered in San Francisco, CA, with
offices in Munich, Germany, and Austin, TX.

For more information, visit \url{https://novapulse.ai}.
