\section{Background}

Over the past quarter, the engineering team has conducted a thorough review of our
current software development practices. Feedback from the Q1 retrospective
sessions identified several areas where our existing workflow introduces
unnecessary friction and delays time-to-market for critical features.

This memo outlines the approved changes to our development process, effective
June 1, 2026.

\section{Proposed Changes}

The following changes have been approved by the engineering leadership team:

\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Sprint Duration:} Reduce sprint length from three weeks to
          two weeks to increase feedback frequency and reduce batch sizes.
    \item \textbf{Code Review Policy:} Require at least one approval from a
          designated code owner for all pull requests merging into the
          \texttt{main} branch.
    \item \textbf{Definition of Done:} Expand the Definition of Done to include
          automated accessibility checks and performance benchmarks for all
          user-facing changes.
    \item \textbf{Incident Post-Mortems:} Transition to blameless post-mortem
          reviews with a mandatory 48-hour turnaround for initial drafts.
\end{enumerate}
