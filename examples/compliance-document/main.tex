\section{PURPOSE AND SCOPE}

This document establishes the compliance framework for Meridian Financial Services, Inc. (``Meridian'' or the ``Company'') with respect to the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS), Version 4.0. This framework applies to all business units, subsidiaries, and third-party service providers that process, store, or transmit cardholder data.

\section{INCIDENT RESPONSE}

\subsection{Incident Response Plan Summary}
Meridian maintains a comprehensive Incident Response Plan (Document ID: IRP-2024-001) that includes:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Preparation} --- Team roles, communication templates, and forensic tools readiness.
    \item \textbf{Detection and Analysis} --- Monitoring, alerting, and triage procedures.
    \item \textbf{Containment} --- Short-term and long-term containment strategies.
    \item \textbf{Eradication and Recovery} --- Root cause analysis and system restoration procedures.
    \item \textbf{Post-Incident Activity} --- Lessons learned documentation and control improvements.
\end{enumerate}
