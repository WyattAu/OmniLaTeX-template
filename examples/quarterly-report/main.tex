\section*{Executive Summary}

Acme Corporation delivered strong results in Q2 2026, with consolidated
revenue of \$48.3M representing 14\% year-over-year growth. Operating margin
improved to 22.4\%, driven by infrastructure cost optimization and favorable
product mix. The enterprise segment continues to outperform, contributing 62\%
of total revenue.

Key achievements this quarter include the successful launch of the Acme
Platform v3.0, expansion into the APAC market with two new customer wins, and
the reduction of customer churn to 3.1\% from 4.8\% in Q1.

\section{Key Metrics}

\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR):} \$187.2M, up 16\% year-over-year.
    \item \textbf{Net Revenue Retention:} 118\%, reflecting strong upsell and
          cross-sell execution.
    \item \textbf{Customer Count:} 2,840 active accounts, a net addition of 185
          in Q2.
    \item \textbf{Average Contract Value:} \$65,900, up 9\% from Q2 2025.
    \item \textbf{Customer Churn:} 3.1\%, improved from 4.8\% in Q1 2026.
    \item \textbf{Employee Headcount:} 612 full-time equivalents, a net addition
          of 28 in Q2.
\end{itemize}

\section{Operational Highlights}

\subsection{Product}

The Acme Platform v3.0 launched on May 15, 2026, introducing real-time
analytics dashboards, enhanced API rate limits, and improved SSO integration.
Early adoption metrics are promising:

\begin{itemize}
    \item 34\% of enterprise customers activated v3.0 within the first 30 days.
    \item API call volume increased 22\% following the rate limit improvements.
    \item Customer-reported feature requests decreased 15\% due to expanded
          native capabilities.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Market Expansion}

The APAC expansion initiative secured two enterprise deals during Q2:

\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Nippon Technologies} (Tokyo): \$1.2M annual contract for the
          full platform suite.
    \item \textbf{Sydney Health Systems} (Sydney): \$800K annual contract focused
          on the analytics module.
\end{itemize}

\section{Outlook}

Management reaffirms full-year 2026 guidance:

\begin{itemize}
    \item Revenue: \$190M -- \$195M (12--15\% growth).
    \item Operating Margin: 21--23\%.
    \item Capital Expenditures: \$15M -- \$18M, primarily for data center
          expansion in APAC.
\end{itemize}

Q3 priorities include the general availability of the Acme Platform v3.1
(advanced workflow automation), continued APAC market development, and the
launch of a partner certification program to accelerate channel sales.
