\section*{President's Message}

Dear Neighbors,

Summer is finally here, and with it comes a full calendar of community events.
This month we are thrilled to announce the opening of the new community garden
behind the recreation center, a project two years in the making. Thank you to
the volunteer committee who made this possible.

We also want to remind everyone about the updated parking guidelines effective
July 1. Please review the details in the Community Updates section.

Wishing you a wonderful summer,
\vspace{0.3em}

\noindent\textbf{Margaret Chen}\\
President, Greenfield HOA

\section*{Community Garden Grand Opening}

The Greenfield Community Garden will hold its grand opening on \textbf{Saturday,
June 21 at 10:00~AM}. The ceremony will include a ribbon-cutting with Mayor
López, followed by plot assignments for registered gardeners.

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*, nosep]
    \item 40 individual plots available (4$\times$8 ft each)
    \item Shared tool shed and composting station
    \item Wheelchair-accessible raised beds
    \item Water hookups at every fourth plot
\end{itemize}

Contact garden@greenfield-hoa.org to reserve your plot.

\section*{Community Updates}

\subsection*{Parking Guidelines}

Effective July 1, overnight street parking between 2:00--6:00~AM will require
a residential permit. Permits are free and can be obtained at the HOA office or
online at greenfield-hoa.org/parking.

\subsection*{Pool Season}

The community pool opens May 24 and operates daily from 6 AM--10 PM.
Lap swim is available 6--8 AM weekdays. Lifeguard certifications are still
available for residents ages 16+.
