\section*{Dear Stakeholders}

NovaPulse Technologies delivered another strong quarter, achieving record
revenue of \$12.8M in Q2 2026 and surpassing the \$50M ARR milestone
for the first time. Customer growth accelerated with 42 new logos added
during the quarter, driven by strong enterprise demand in North America
and early traction from our European expansion.

Our multi-region platform (Project Atlas) is on track for a November
launch, with beta customers already reporting sub-80ms latency from
EU data centers. We closed a landmark seven-figure deal with Meridian
Healthcare, the largest single contract in company history, and expanded
our partnership with Siemens Digital Industries to co-develop next-generation
predictive maintenance solutions.

\section{Risk Factors}

\begin{itemize}
    \item Macroeconomic uncertainty may delay enterprise procurement cycles.
        Mitigation: 78\% of ARR under multi-year contracts.
    \item Competitive pressure from cloud hyperscalers bundling AI capabilities.
        Mitigation: differentiated by vertical expertise, edge inference, and
        compliance depth.
    \item Project Atlas execution risk. Mitigation: experienced team,
        phased rollout, weekly executive reviews.
    \item Regulatory changes (EU AI Act). Mitigation: dedicated compliance
        team, privacy-by-design architecture.
\end{itemize}
