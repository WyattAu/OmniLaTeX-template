\begin{abstract}
This technical data sheet provides comprehensive physical, chemical, and
performance specifications for TITANFLOW-3000, a high-performance hydraulic
fluid engineered for aerospace and industrial applications. TITANFLOW-3000
meets or exceeds MIL-PRF-83282, ISO 15380 Type HEES, and Airbus AIMS 09-10
specifications. The fluid is based on a synthetic ester base stock with
proprietary additive technology for enhanced oxidative stability, anti-wear
protection, and low-temperature fluidity. This document covers product
description, compositional information, physical properties, performance
characteristics, handling and storage requirements, and regulatory compliance.
\end{abstract}

\subsection{Wear Protection}

Four-ball wear and FZG scuffing test results demonstrate excellent anti-wear
performance under boundary lubrication conditions.

\begin{equation}
    \text{Wear Rate} = \frac{\Delta V}{F_N \cdot s}
    \label{eq:wear-rate}
\end{equation}

where $\Delta V$ is the wear volume, $F_N$ is the normal load, and $s$ is
the sliding distance. The wear rate for TITANFLOW-3000 was measured at
$2.1 \times 10^{-8}$ mm$^3$/(N$\cdot$m) in four-ball testing at 75\degree C
and 392 N load.

\section{Handling and Storage}
\label{sec:handling}

\subsection{Handling Precautions}

\begin{itemize}
    \item Avoid contact with eyes and prolonged skin contact. Use chemical
          safety goggles and nitrile gloves when handling.
    \item In case of skin contact, wash with soap and water. Do not use
          solvents for skin decontamination.
    \item Avoid ingestion. If ingested, do not induce vomiting; seek
          immediate medical attention.
    \item Prevent spills. Use drip trays under equipment during
          fluid changes.
    \item Do not reuse contaminated containers.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Storage Requirements}

\begin{itemize}
    \item Store in original, sealed containers at temperatures between
          $-20\degree$C and $+50\degree$C.
    \item Protect from direct sunlight and moisture.
    \item Containers should be stored on level surfaces with secondary
          containment.
    \item Shelf life: 5 years from date of manufacture when stored in
          original sealed containers. After opening, use within 2 years.
    \item Re-test after shelf life expiration: sample should be tested
          for viscosity, TAN, and water content before use.
\end{itemize}

\section{Disposal and Environmental Considerations}
\label{sec:disposal}

\begin{itemize}
    \item TITANFLOW-3000 is readily biodegradable ($>$ 60\% in 28 days,
          OECD 301B).
    \item Low aquatic toxicity: LC$_{50}$ (fish, 96h) $>$ 100 mg/L;
          EC$_{50}$ (Daphnia, 48h) $>$ 100 mg/L.
    \item Dispose of used fluid in accordance with local, regional, and
          national regulations. Contact a licensed waste disposal facility.
    \item Do not discharge into drains, surface water, or groundwater.
    \item Used TITANFLOW-3000 can be reconditioned through filtration and
          re-additization at approved facilities.
\end{itemize}
