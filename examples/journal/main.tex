\section{Methodology}
Describe the experimental setup or theoretical approach~\cite{diracPrinciplesQuantumMechanics1981}.
We model the system dynamics using the Hamiltonian
\begin{equation}
    \hat{H} = -\frac{\hbar^{2}}{2m}\nabla^{2} + V(\mathbf{r}),
    \label{eq:hamiltonian}
\end{equation}
where $V(\mathbf{r})$ is the external potential and $m$ is the effective mass.
The energy eigenvalues $E_n$ satisfy
\begin{equation}
    \hat{H}\,\psi_n(\mathbf{r}) = E_n\,\psi_n(\mathbf{r}),
\end{equation}
and are obtained numerically via a finite-difference discretisation on a
$256 \times 256$ grid.
