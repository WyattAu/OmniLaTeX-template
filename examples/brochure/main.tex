\section*{Welcome to Horizon}

At Horizon Wellness Center, we believe that true health encompasses body, mind,
and spirit. Our integrated approach combines evidence-based medicine with
holistic therapies in a warm, supportive environment.

Whether you are recovering from an injury, managing a chronic condition, or
simply seeking to optimize your well-being, our team of certified professionals
is here to guide you every step of the way.

\section*{Our Services}

\subsection*{Physical Therapy}
Licensed therapists provide personalized rehabilitation programs using manual
therapy, therapeutic exercise, and modalities such as ultrasound and electrical
stimulation. We specialize in orthopedic, neurological, and pediatric
rehabilitation.

\subsection*{Massage Therapy}
Choose from Swedish, deep tissue, sports, or trigger point massage. Our licensed
massage therapists tailor each session to address your specific needs and
preferences.

\columnbreak

\subsection*{Nutrition Counseling}
Our registered dietitians offer one-on-one consultations, meal planning, and
ongoing support for weight management, sports nutrition, food allergies, and
medical nutrition therapy.

\subsection*{Mindfulness \& Yoga}
Daily group classes led by certified instructors. Sessions range from gentle
restorative yoga to vigorous vinyasa flow, with guided meditation available
every morning at 7 AM.

\subsection*{Aquatic Therapy}
Our heated indoor pool hosts therapy sessions and fitness classes. Aquatic
exercise is ideal for joint-friendly rehabilitation and cardiovascular conditioning.
