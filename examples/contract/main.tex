\section*{SERVICE AGREEMENT}

This Service Agreement (``Agreement'') is entered into as of \today\ by and between:

\textbf{Provider:} TechNova Solutions Inc., a Delaware corporation with principal offices at 456 Innovation Drive, San Francisco, CA 94105 (``Provider'').

\textbf{Client:} Meridian Healthcare Systems, a Massachusetts corporation with principal offices at 789 Medical Center Blvd, Boston, MA 02115 (``Client'').

Provider and Client may be referred to individually as a ``Party'' and collectively as the ``Parties.''

\section{RECITALS}

WHEREAS, Provider is engaged in the business of providing custom software development, integration, and maintenance services;

WHEREAS, Client desires to engage Provider to perform certain services related to the development and deployment of a clinical data management platform (the ``Project'');

WHEREAS, the Parties desire to set forth the terms and conditions under which Provider will perform such services;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and promises contained herein, the Parties agree as follows:

\section{SCOPE OF SERVICES}

Provider shall perform the following services (the ``Services''):

\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Requirements Gathering and Analysis} --- Provider shall conduct workshops with Client stakeholders to elicit, document, and validate functional and non-functional requirements for the clinical data management platform.

    \item \textbf{System Design and Architecture} --- Provider shall produce detailed technical specifications, system architecture documents, database schemas, and API interface definitions for Client review and approval.

    \item \textbf{Software Development} --- Provider shall develop, test, and deliver all software components as specified in the approved requirements documentation, including but not limited to the web application, RESTful APIs, data import/export modules, and reporting engine.

    \item \textbf{Quality Assurance and Testing} --- Provider shall perform unit testing, integration testing, system testing, and user acceptance testing in accordance with the approved test plan.

    \item \textbf{Deployment and Migration} --- Provider shall deploy the completed system to Client's production environment and assist with data migration from existing systems.

    \item \textbf{Training and Documentation} --- Provider shall deliver comprehensive user manuals, administrator guides, and conduct training sessions for Client personnel.

    \item \textbf{Warranty Support} --- Provider shall provide defect resolution support for a period of twelve (12) months following final acceptance.
\end{enumerate}

\section{TERM AND TERMINATION}

\subsection{Initial Term}
This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and shall continue for a period of eighteen (18) months, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Section.

\subsection{Renewal}
Following the Initial Term, this Agreement shall automatically renew for successive twelve (12) month periods unless either Party provides written notice of non-renewal at least sixty (60) days prior to the expiration of the then-current term.

\subsection{Termination for Cause}
Either Party may terminate this Agreement upon written notice if the other Party:
\begin{enumerate}[label=(\alph*)]
    \item commits a material breach that remains uncured for thirty (30) days after written notice; or
    \item becomes insolvent, files for bankruptcy, or makes an assignment for the benefit of creditors.
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{Termination for Convenience}
Either Party may terminate this Agreement for convenience upon ninety (90) days' written notice to the other Party, provided Client pays for all Services rendered through the effective termination date.

\subsection{Effect of Termination}
Upon termination, Provider shall: (i) cease all work under this Agreement; (ii) deliver all completed and in-progress work product to Client; (iii) return or destroy all Client confidential information; and (iv) cooperate with Client in transitioning Services to an alternative provider.

\subsection{Payment Terms}
\begin{enumerate}
    \item Provider shall submit itemized invoices to Client on a monthly basis.
    \item Client shall pay each invoice within thirty (30) days of receipt.

\end{enumerate}

\subsection{Budget Cap}
Total fees under this Agreement shall not exceed \$1,250,000 without prior written approval from Client's authorized representative.

\subsection{Definition}

\subsection{Obligations}
