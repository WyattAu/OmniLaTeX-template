\section*{Project Summary}
This proposal outlines a research plan for developing novel approaches to
distributed systems.  The proposed work addresses critical challenges in
scalability, fault tolerance, and performance optimisation for
next-generation cloud infrastructure.

\section*{Project Description}

\subsection{Introduction and Background}
The rapid growth of cloud computing has created new demands on distributed
systems~\cite{bibid}.  Current approaches struggle to balance consistency
with availability under partition conditions, as described by the CAP
theorem.  This research aims to develop new algorithms that provide
stronger consistency guarantees while maintaining high availability.

\subsection{Proposed Research}
Our approach builds on recent advances in consensus protocols and
crash-replicated state machines.  We propose a novel hybrid protocol
that combines the strengths of Paxos and Raft while addressing their
individual limitations.
